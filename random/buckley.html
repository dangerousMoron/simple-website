Whence and what art thou, execrable shape, That darst, though grim and terrible, advance Thy miscreated Front athwart my way To yonder Gates

translates to:

What are you, and where do you come from, you foul shape, That dares if even if you are grim and horrible, to place Your bastardised figure across my path To those gates? ... (I mean to go through them)
